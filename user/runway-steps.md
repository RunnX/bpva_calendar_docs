# Runway Step Configurations

## Overview

The Runway Step Configurations feature helps pole vaulters track and manage their approach run settings. Record your step counts, measurements, and pole specifications for different runway configurations. Perfect for tracking what works best for you and maintaining consistency across practices and competitions.

## Features

### Step Management
- **Multiple Steps**: Add as many steps as needed for your approach
- **Custom Measurements**: Record exact distances for each step
- **Step Numbers**: Organize steps sequentially
- **Flexible Editing**: Update configurations as you progress

### Pole Information
- **Optional Pole Data**: Add pole specs for each step (optional)
- **Weight Tracking**: Record pole weight
- **Length Recording**: Note pole length
- **Grip Notes**: Track grip height or other grip details
- **Quick Reference**: See pole info at a glance

### Configuration Tools
- **Drag & Drop Reordering**: Easily reorganize steps
- **Auto-Sorting**: Steps automatically sort by number
- **Edit Anytime**: Update measurements and pole info
- **Delete Steps**: Remove outdated configurations
- **Save Automatically**: Changes sync to your account

## How to Use

### Adding Your First Step

1. **Navigate to Runway Steps**
   - Open hamburger menu (â˜°)
   - Select **"Runway Steps"**
   - View your configuration screen

2. **Add New Step**
   - Tap **"Add Step"** button (+ icon)
   - Enter step information:
     - **Step Number**: Which step (e.g., 3, 4, 5, 6)
     - **Measurement**: Distance in feet/meters
   - Tap **Save**

3. **View Your Step**
   - Step appears in your list
   - Shows step number and measurement
   - Expandable card for more details

### Adding Pole Information

1. **Open Step Details**
   - Tap on any existing step to expand
   - View current step information

2. **Add/Edit Pole**
   - Tap **"Edit"** icon
   - Scroll to **Pole Information** section
   - Fill in pole details:
     - **Weight**: Pole weight (e.g., "150 lbs")
     - **Length**: Pole length (e.g., "13'6\"")
     - **Grip**: Grip height or notes (e.g., "Top grip" or "12'9\"")
   - All pole fields are optional
   - Tap **Save**

3. **View Pole Info**
   - Pole details shown in expanded step card
   - Organized and easy to read
   - Update anytime as you try new poles

### Managing Multiple Steps

#### Adding More Steps

1. Continue adding steps for your full approach:
   - 3-step approach
   - 4-step approach
   - 5-step approach
   - 6-step or longer
   
2. Each step can have:
   - Unique measurement
   - Different pole if needed
   - Specific notes in pole fields

#### Editing Existing Steps

1. **Select Step to Edit**
   - Tap on step card to expand
   - Tap **Edit** icon (pencil)

2. **Update Information**
   - Change step number
   - Modify measurement
   - Update pole information
   - Tap **Save** when done

3. **Changes Sync**
   - Updates save to your account
   - Available across devices
   - Maintained in cloud storage

#### Deleting Steps

1. **Remove Unwanted Step**
   - Tap on step to expand
   - Tap **Delete** icon (trash)
   - Confirm deletion

2. **Step Removed**
   - Deleted from your configuration
   - Cannot be undone
   - Other steps remain intact

### Reordering Steps

1. **Drag and Drop**
   - Press and hold on step card
   - Drag to new position
   - Release to drop
   - Steps automatically renumber

2. **Auto-Sorting**
   - Steps sort by step number
   - Keeps configuration organized
   - Maintains logical order

## Understanding Step Configurations

### Step Number
- **Purpose**: Identifies which step in your approach
- **Common Values**: 3, 4, 5, 6, 7, 8
- **Progression**: Track different approach lengths
- **Competition**: Know which step for different heights

### Measurement
- **Distance**: How far back you start
- **Units**: Feet, meters, or custom
- **Precision**: Record exact measurements
- **Consistency**: Use same measurement system
- **Coach Guidance**: Work with coach to find right distances

### Pole Information

#### Weight
- **Purpose**: Track pole stiffness/rating
- **Format**: Typically in pounds (lbs)
- **Example**: "150 lbs", "160 lbs", "12.7"
- **Progression**: Move up as you improve
- **Multiple Poles**: Different weights for different steps

#### Length
- **Purpose**: Record pole length
- **Format**: Feet and inches or metric
- **Example**: "13'6\"", "14'0\"", "4.2m"
- **Standard Lengths**: Common: 13', 13'6", 14', 14'6", 15'
- **Step Dependent**: Longer steps may use longer poles

#### Grip
- **Flexible Field**: Use for any grip-related notes
- **Grip Height**: Where you hold ("12'9\"", "top grip")
- **Hand Position**: Notes about hand placement
- **Adjustments**: Track grip changes
- **Example**: "Top grip", "12'9\" grip", "Hand at 13'"

## Best Practices

### Accurate Recording
- **Measure Precisely**: Use exact measurements
- **Verify with Coach**: Confirm distances are correct
- **Update Regularly**: Keep configuration current
- **Record Immediately**: Add new configs right away
- **Document Changes**: Update when you change poles/steps

### Organization
- **Logical Step Numbers**: Use actual step counts
- **Consistent Units**: Stick to feet or meters
- **Clear Pole Notes**: Make pole info easy to understand
- **Delete Old Configs**: Remove outdated/unused steps
- **Regular Review**: Check accuracy periodically

### Experimentation
- **Try Different Steps**: Experiment with approach length
- **Test Pole Combinations**: Try different poles at different steps
- **Track Results**: Note what works in journal
- **Gradual Changes**: Don't change everything at once
- **Coach Input**: Work with coaches on modifications

### Safety
- **Use Appropriate Poles**: Don't exceed safe weight ratings
- **Follow Coach Guidance**: Coaches know safe progressions
- **Update When Growing**: Adjust as you get stronger/taller
- **Don't Rush**: Progress at safe pace

## Troubleshooting

### Steps Not Saving

**Check Internet Connection:**
- Ensure Wi-Fi or cellular data enabled
- Configuration syncs to cloud
- May need to retry if connection lost

**Verify Login:**
- Must be signed in to save
- Sign out and sign back in
- Check account status

**App Restart:**
- Close and reopen app
- Check if changes persisted
- Contact coach if issue continues

### Reordering Not Working

**Drag and Drop:**
- Press and hold on step card
- Wait for card to "lift"
- Drag to new position
- Make sure you're on steps list (not in edit mode)

**Auto-Sorting:**
- Steps sort by step number automatically
- Change step number to change order
- Can't manually override number-based sorting

### Pole Information Not Showing

**Expand Step Card:**
- Tap on step to expand
- Pole info shown in expanded view
- Collapsed view only shows step number/measurement

**Add Pole Data:**
- Tap Edit on step
- Scroll to Pole Information
- Fill in fields
- Save changes

### Lost Configurations

**Cloud Sync:**
- Configurations stored in cloud (Firestore)
- Sign in to same account on any device
- Data should sync automatically

**Different Account:**
- Configurations tied to your user account
- Verify you're signed into correct account
- Can't transfer between accounts

**Deleted by Accident:**
- Deletion is permanent
- No undo option
- Re-enter configuration if needed
- Consider taking screenshots of important configs

## Need Help?

### Common Questions

**Q: How many steps should I add?**
A: Work with your coach. Most vaulters use 3-6 step approaches depending on skill level.

**Q: What if I use different poles on same step?**
A: Create separate step entries to track different pole options for same step count.

**Q: Can I share my configurations with teammates?**
A: Not automatically. You can show them your phone or send screenshots.

**Q: What units should I use for measurements?**
A: Use whatever your facility/coaches use. Stay consistent with one system.

**Q: Do I need to fill in all pole information?**
A: No, pole information is completely optional. Add what's useful to you.
