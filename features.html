Features Overview

The BPVA Calendar App is packed with features designed to enhance your training experience and keep you connected with the Buckeye Pole Vault Academy community.

üìÖ Event Calendar

View Your Schedule

  • Multiple View Options - Switch between month and list calendar views
  • Event Details - Tap any event to see full details including time, location, and description
  • Read-Only Access - View academy events selected by administrators from Google Calendar
  • Real-Time Sync - Events automatically sync from the academy's Google Calendar

Calendar Content

  • Training schedules and sessions
  • Academy events and announcements
  • Synchronized from Google Calendar by admins

Note: Users cannot create, edit, or delete calendar events. All calendar management is performed by administrators.

üîî Push Notifications & Messages

Stay informed with in-app messaging:

  • Admin Announcements - Important messages from academy administrators
  • Message Types - Info, Warning, Urgent, and Announcement priority levels
  • Push Notifications - Receive alerts when new messages are posted
  • Message History - View and track all messages in the app
  • Read Status - Track which messages you've read

Message Management

Control your message experience:

  • View all messages in the Messages section
  • Dismiss messages after reading
  • Push notifications controlled through device settings

Gym Check-In

  • Location-Verified Check-In - Check in when you arrive at the gym
  • GPS Verification - Location services verify you're at the academy
  • Attendance Tracking - Track your gym visits over time

Runway Configuration

  • Personal Runway Steps - Configure your runway starting positions
  • Step Management - Add, edit, and reorder your runway marks

Training Journal

  • Practice Notes - Record thoughts about training sessions
  • Progress Tracking - Document achievements and goals
  • Personal Records - Keep notes on technique, conditions, and performance

Family Account Management

üèÉ Receptionist Features

Front Desk Check-In System

  • Quick Athlete Search - Search members by name or email
  • One-Tap Check-In - Check in athletes with a single tap
  • Check-In Status - See who has already checked in today
  • Time-Stamped Records - All check-ins are recorded with date and time

Dedicated Dashboard

  • Athlete-Only List - View only active athletes (no staff or parents)
  • Simplified Interface - Clean, focused UI for front desk use
  • Real-Time Clock - Display current date and time
  • Quick Logout - Easy sign-out for shift changes

Note: Receptionist accounts have no access to calendar, admin features, or athlete-specific functionality. The role is dedicated solely to processing gym check-ins at the front desk.
